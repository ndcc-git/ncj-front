Frequently Asked Questions

What is NCJ? ▼

Notre Dame Cultural Junction (NCJ) is an annual cultural festival organized by the Notre Dame Cultural Club, celebrating art, music, dance, and drama.

When and where is the event? ▼

The 10th NCJ will be held in February 2026 at Notre Dame College. Exact dates and venues will be announced soon.

How do I register? ▼

You can register online through our website by clicking the "Register" button. Choose your event category and fill in the required details.

What are the participation fees? ▼

Participation fees vary by event category. Please check individual event pages for specific fee information.

Can I participate in multiple events? ▼

Yes! Participants are welcome to register for multiple events, subject to scheduling constraints.
