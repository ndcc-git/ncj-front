About the Event

Battle of Bands is the ultimate musical showdown where talented bands compete for glory! Show us your musical prowess, stage presence, and originality. This is your chance to shine on the biggest cultural stage in the country.

Bring your best performance, electrify the crowd, and take home the title of the best band at the 10th NCJ. All genres are welcome - rock, pop, fusion, metal, and more!

Prize Pool: ৳50,000 + Trophy + Certificates

Rules & Regulations

  1. Each team must consist of 4-8 members.
  2. Performance time limit: 15-20 minutes including setup.
  3. Teams must perform at least 2 songs (original or covers).
  4. All lyrics must be appropriate for a college audience.
  5. Teams must bring their own instruments (drums and amps provided).
  6. Sound check will be conducted 30 minutes before the event.
  7. Judges' decision will be final and binding.
  8. Registration fee is non-refundable.
